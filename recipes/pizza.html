Pizza Recipe

Description

A homemade pizza with your favorite toppings, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese (mozzarella, cheddar, or your choice)
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Mushrooms, sliced
  • Green bell peppers, diced
  • Onion, sliced
  • Olives, sliced
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the recommended temperature for the pizza dough.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving some space at the edges for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle cheese over the sauce, then add your desired toppings: pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.
  6. Sprinkle Italian seasoning over the top.
  7. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a minute, slice, and serve!
