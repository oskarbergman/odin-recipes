Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish consisting of layers of pasta, flavorful meat sauce, creamy cheeses, onions, and garlic, all baked to perfection. It's a comforting and satisfying meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onions, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, cook ground beef, onions, and garlic until browned. Add tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with salt and pepper.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Layer lasagna noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish. Repeat the layers until the dish is filled.
  6. Top with Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes or until bubbly and golden.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  9. Serve slices of lasagna and enjoy your delicious homemade meal!
